Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about 3D Estate, NeRF capture, and how the platform works for buyers and professionals.

Getting started
What is 3D Estate?

3D Estate is a platform that lets architects, agents, and designers capture real spaces using NeRF technology and share them as interactive 3D tours that clients can explore from anywhere.

Do I need special equipment to capture a 3D space?

No. Most listings can be captured using just a smartphone camera following our guided capture flow. For larger commercial spaces, we recommend our Pro capture kit for faster processing.

How long does it take to turn photos into a 3D model?

Most residential spaces process within 20–40 minutes after upload. Larger properties or high-resolution captures may take a few hours.

Using the platform
Can I share a 3D tour with someone who doesn't have an account?

Yes. Every 3D tour has a shareable web link that works in any browser, no account or app download required for the viewer.

Can clients change materials or colors while viewing a model?

Yes, if the listing owner enables it. Viewers can preview alternate wall colors, flooring, and finishes directly inside the 3D viewer.

Is my project data private?

By default, all uploaded scans and listings are private and only visible to people you explicitly share a link with. You control visibility for every project.

Billing
Is there a free plan?

Yes, the Starter plan is free and includes up to 3 active 3D listings — perfect for trying out the platform before upgrading.

What payment methods are supported?

We accept major debit/credit cards and bank transfers for Nigerian accounts. Enterprise plans can be invoiced directly.
